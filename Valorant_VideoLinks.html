Valorant is a free-to-play tactical FPS by Riot Games, featuring 5v5 matches where players choose unique Agents with special abilities. One team attacks by planting a Spike, while the other defends. The game emphasizes precise gunplay, strategy, and teamwork. With regular updates, new content, and a strong esports scene, Valorant remains a top competitive shooter.
